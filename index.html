01 — About Me

The mind behind
the research

My name is Arya Mohan G, and I am a student specialising in M.Sc Data Science and Bio AI. I have always been fascinated by how technology can be used to understand the natural world.

My studies focus on using data-driven methods to solve complex biological challenges. I believe that the combination of biology and artificial intelligence holds the key to future medical breakthroughs.

I am particularly interested in how machine learning can speed up drug discovery and development. My goal is to use my programming skills to help doctors provide better and more personalised treatments.

I enjoy working with complex algorithms that can predict biological outcomes with high accuracy. Data Science gives me the tools to turn raw information into useful knowledge — and Bio AI lets me apply those tools to the most important subject of all: human life.

I am constantly learning about new developments in neural networks and genomics to stay at the cutting edge. I aim to bridge the gap between computer science and laboratory research, motivated every day by the possibility of making a positive impact on global health.
